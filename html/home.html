75th Anniversary: Giallo Modena Special Edition

Ferrari celebrates the challenges and achievements over 75 years of history with the Giallo Modena Special Edition, a series of limited-edition items in yellow. Emblematic of the indomitable spirit and timeless style of the Maranello house, the color yellow evokes memories that will remain etched in the history of the legend, but is also the connection with the coat of arms of the city of Modena where it all began.
